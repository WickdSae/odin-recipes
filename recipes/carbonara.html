Carbonara alla Francese

Pasta alla Carbonara is an Italian traditionnal dish, originally composed of fresh pasta, parmesan cheese and sliced bacon. This french version cuts half the cheese and replaces it with cream.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Pasta
  • Diced bacon
  • Fresh Cream
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook you Fresh pasta
  2. Dice your garlic, and bacon, bake it all together
  3. Once grilled, add the cream and pepper
  4. Dress your plates and add the parmesan cheese
